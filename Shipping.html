Shipping & Delivery Policy

Information about our delivery timelines and processes for physical goods.

1. Digital Delivery

For electronic tickets, vouchers, and confirmations, delivery is immediate upon successful payment. These will be sent to the email address provided during booking. Please check your spam folder if you don't receive them within 24 hours.

2. Physical Documents

For physical tickets, travel documents, or merchandise (if applicable):

  • Standard delivery within India: 3-5 business days
  • Express delivery within India: 1-2 business days
  • International delivery: 7-14 business days (varies by destination)

Delivery times are estimates and may vary due to factors beyond our control.

3. Shipping Methods

We use reputable courier services including but not limited to India Post, Blue Dart, DHL, and FedEx. The shipping method will be selected based on the urgency and destination of your order.

4. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs vary depending on the delivery method and destination. These will be clearly displayed during checkout before you complete your purchase. Some promotions may include free shipping.

5. Tracking Your Order

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this to track your package on the courier's website.

6. Delivery Address

Please ensure your delivery address is accurate and complete. We are not responsible for delays or non-delivery due to incorrect addresses provided by customers. Address changes after shipment may not be possible.

7. Failed Delivery Attempts

If delivery attempts fail due to recipient unavailability, the package may be returned to us. Additional shipping fees may apply for re-delivery.

8. International Shipping

For international shipments, customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or fees imposed by the destination country. These are not included in our shipping charges.

9. Delivery of Services

For travel services (flights, hotels, tours), delivery means the completion of booking confirmation and provision of necessary documentation. The actual service will be provided by the respective service providers at the specified dates.

10. Contact for Delivery Issues

For any issues with delivery, please contact our customer support at support@eziotravels.com or call +91 12345 67890 within 3 business days of expected delivery date.
